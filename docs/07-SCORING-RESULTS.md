# Interpret Scores, Confidence, and Results

## Rubric

The seven categories are Clear Explanation (cap 20), Problem (15), Solution (15), Audience (15), Evidence (15), Communication (10), and Time Management (10). Category values floor at zero and overall score caps at 100.

Result levels use inclusive minimums: Seedling 0, Sprouting 40, Growing 60, and Thriving 80. Confidence starts at the scenario value (55 for Smart School Garden) and clamps to 0-100. Strength thresholds are 12/9/9/9/9/6/6 in rubric order; ties remain deterministic.

## Results screen

The implemented screen presents overall score, final confidence, pitching and communications rollups, two strengths, two improvements, and up to six ordered question-review items with selected response, feedback, and explanation. Retry clears attempt data. Failed completion submission preserves the result for resubmission.

## Interpretation limits

Scores are authored formative feedback, not a validated psychometric instrument, grade, certification, or prediction of real investment performance. Learning and assessment owners must approve rubric changes. Do not compare learners or infer sensitive traits from these values.

## Change checklist

Update score caps, thresholds, localization, content deltas, result tests, completion competency mapping, and documentation together. Maintain stable category IDs once a production LMS mapping is approved.
