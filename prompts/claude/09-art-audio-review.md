# Review Art and Audio Readiness

## Objective

Assess visual coherence, child suitability, provenance, reduced motion, contrast, and the plan for nine licensed audio cues.

## Allowed Files

Read `Assets/Art/CREDITS.md`, manifest, audio placeholder guide, importer/view tests, and docs 08-09. Suggest documentation/cue briefs only.

## Files Not To Change

Do not generate/replace assets, claim licenses, alter import metadata/scenes, or inspect external repositories. Never inspect or modify the external AGROVATOR LMS repository.

## Required Output

Provide an asset inventory, provenance gaps, visual/audio human-review checklist, cue briefs, and accessibility risks.

## Required Tests

List required import, scene containment, contrast, reduced-motion, browser unlock, volume, loudness, and fallback checks; distinguish existing evidence from pending checks.

## Definition of Done

No placeholder is represented as final, rights claims cite records, and creative/accessibility approvals remain human-owned.
